Metal Metal Podcast

Welcome to the Metal Metal Podcast website. This podcast was founded by metal fans Jeff Scott Brown and Matt Taylor. We've been damaging our eardrums with thrashing heavy metal for decades.

In each podcast, we'll explore our metal libraries to bring you hidden gems of pounding, chunky, raw walls of sound. Jeff is an aficionado of classic bands like Black Sabbath and Slayer, but his knowledge of the metal genre is broad and deep. Progressive rock and heavy metal is Jeff's forte. Matt gets his metal kicks from droning heavy stoner and doom metal. Whether it's the unrelenting hum of Sleep or the raw power of Clutch, he'll bring you a serious metal fix.

But we're not one trick ponies. Get ready to hear us explore all genres of metal.
